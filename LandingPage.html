Our Mission: A Voice for Every Indian

Visora was born from a simple yet powerful idea: **communication should be universal and barrier-free.** In a country as linguistically diverse as India, ensuring accessibility means addressing both spoken vernaculars and crucial languages like Indian Sign Language (ISL).

  • **Inclusivity Focus:** Targeting the deaf and hard-of-hearing community with specialized ISL interpretation models.
  • **Vernacular Support:** Comprehensive translation for numerous Indian regional languages, facilitating smoother daily interactions.
  • **Commitment to Open Source:** Building trust and enabling community contributions to constantly refine and expand our models.
